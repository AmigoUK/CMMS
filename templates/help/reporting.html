Reporting a Problem

Anyone can report a maintenance problem. There are two ways: scan a QR code on the equipment, or log in and submit manually.

{# ── Scanning a QR Code ────────────────────────────────────── #}
Scanning a QR Code
  1. Find the QR code label on the equipment (usually a small sticker)
  2. Open your phone camera and point it at the QR code
  3. Tap the link that appears — it opens the report form
  4. The equipment is automatically identified — no need to search for it
  5. Fill in what's wrong and submit
If anonymous reporting is enabled, you can submit without logging in. Otherwise, you'll be asked to sign in first.
{# ── Submitting Manually ───────────────────────────────────── #}
Submitting Manually
  1. Sign in to CMMS
  2. Click "Report a Problem" on the dashboard
  3. Fill in the title (what's wrong), description (details), and priority
  4. Select the asset/property item (required) — start typing 3 or more characters to search the list, then pick the matching item
  5. Optionally select the location
  6. Attach a photo if helpful
  7. Click "Submit Request"
{# ── Priority Levels ───────────────────────────────────────── #}
Priority Levels
Priority When to use
Not urgent Cosmetic issues, minor inconveniences. No impact on operations.
Normal Should be addressed within a few days. Operations can continue.
Urgent Significant impact. Workaround may exist but needs prompt attention.
Emergency Operations stopped or safety risk. Needs immediate action.
Tip: When in doubt, choose "Normal". A supervisor will adjust the priority if needed.
{# ── Tips for a Good Report ─────────────────────────────────── #}
Tips for a Good Report
  • Be specific about what you see, hear, or smell
  • Mention when the problem started and if it's getting worse
  • Include the location if you know it
  • Attach a photo — a picture is worth a thousand words
  • Don't worry about technical terms — describe it in your own words
